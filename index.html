Game Rules

Welcome to the Puzzle GAI. Your objective is clear:

  • **Objective:** Solve a sequence of encrypted puzzles to complete the narrative.
  • **Input:** All solutions must be entered into the "Submit Code" field. Solutions are **case-insensitive** and ignore extra spaces/punctuation.
  • **Difficulty:** Your chosen difficulty determines the **number of puzzles** (Easy: 7, Medium: 5, Hard: 3) and the **complexity** of the ciphers and riddles.
  • **Hints:** You have a maximum of **3 hints per puzzle**. Each hint is more direct than the last. Once you use the final hint, the **Show Answer** option will appear.
  • **Failure:** Using the **Show Answer** button will reveal the current puzzle's solution and immediately end the current game session.
  • **Success:** Solve all puzzles to complete the story and reach the final scoring screen.
